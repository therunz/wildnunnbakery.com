Our Story

Born From
Real Bread

The Wild Nunn Bakery started exactly how the best things do — not from a business plan, but from a genuine obsession. Weekend baking turned into neighbors asking for loaves. Loaves turned into a licensed cottage bakery right here in Indiana.

Everything is made by hand, in small batches, with real ingredients and real fermentation time. No shortcuts. No preservatives. No setting an alarm for Sunday morning — because we don't rest on Sunday.

We operate under Indiana's Cottage Food Law, which means our food comes from an actual home kitchen, made by an actual person who genuinely cares. Pickup is Sunday noon to 2pm. Orders close Thursday. Don't miss the window.
