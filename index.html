Our Story

Born From
Real Bread

The Wild Nunn Bakery started exactly how the best things do — not from a business plan, but from a genuine obsession. Weekend baking turned into neighbors asking for loaves. Loaves turned into a licensed cottage bakery right here in Indiana.

Everything is made by hand, in small batches, with real ingredients and real fermentation time. No shortcuts. No preservatives. No sleeping in on Sunday — because we don't rest on Sunday.

We operate under Indiana's Cottage Food guidelines, which means our food comes from an actual home kitchen, made by an actual person who genuinely cares. Pickup is Sunday noon to 2pm. Orders close Thursday at 8pm. Don't miss the window.

🌾 Craving something you don't see here?

Our menu is alive and we're always down to experiment. Got a wild idea, a flavor obsession, or a deeply specific vision for what sourdough could be? Tell us. The worst we can say is "not yet." The best we can say is "pick up Sunday."
