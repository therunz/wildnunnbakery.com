Our Story

A Passion
Baked In

The Wild Nunn Bakery was born from a deep love of real bread — the kind made with patience, good flour, and wild yeast that's been alive longer than you'd expect. What started as weekend baking turned into something neighbors began asking for, and now we're proud to bring that same care to yours.

We're a home bakery licensed under Indiana's Cottage Food Law, which means everything is baked in small batches right here in our kitchen. You won't find preservatives or shortcuts — just wholesome, handcrafted food made the way it's meant to be.

Based in Indiana and ready to connect with our community, one loaf at a time.
